    Main Menu

    From The Main Menu You Can Play The Game, View The Instructions, Or Quit The Game By Selecting The Approprate Button

    Instruction Menu

    Can View This Page By Clicking Instructions On The Menu Screen. Explains The Rules And Goals Of Shoot-N-Stack.

    In Game

    This Is The View You Will See Shortly After Loading Into The Game. Note That The Missile Ammunition Selected Can be Viewed In The Top Left.

    Pause Menu

    Once In Game Press esc or p To Pause The Game. From There, You Can Either Resume Or Quit The Game.

    Missile In Travel

    All Missile Types Have A 1 Second Cooldown As Can Be Seen On The Bar Beside The Turret That Fired The Missile.

    Game Over Menu

    Once A Piece Has Reached The Top Line, The Game Ends And You Are Presented With This Screen. You Can Quit Or Play Again.
